\chapter{INTRODUCTION}

In recent years, the intersection of healthcare and technology has led to the proliferation of personal health management tools. Among these, menstrual cycle tracking applications have become essential for millions of menstruating individuals worldwide. However, many existing solutions lack personalization or are cluttered with unnecessary features. Bloomcycle is entirely designed around the core needs of users—providing a secure, straightforward, and informative platform to log period dates, document bodily symptoms, and receive AI-driven timeline predictions.
\\\\
Built on the robust MERN stack, Bloomcycle ensures a scalable and dynamic user experience. The application aims to destigmatize reproductive health tracking by offering a clean, friendly user interface where users can confidently input sensitive data. By organizing this data into meaningful formats, such as calendar views and statistical insights, Bloomcycle encourages a proactive approach to personal health management.
